THE WAR

Two squadrons fight an attrition war over a procedurally generated island: VIPERS (your side, green squares) against BANDITS (yellow diamonds). The dead stay dead. When one team is wiped out the winner banner lights up and two fresh squadrons take the sky — the scoreboard on the radar carries over: green = bandits downed, yellow = vipers lost, white = your personal kills.

A jet takes 2–5 cannon hits or one missile; the wreck breaks into its polygons, falls, and burns on the ground. You can soak 8 hits — the aiming ring flashes red and the damage counter on the radar climbs. Watch the tapes: the red line on the speed tape is the stall, the one on the altitude tape is the terrain right below you.

THE PANEL

Left MFD — radar (heading-up): you are the centre tick, blips are jets (IFF-coloured, a dot above/below = higher/lower than you), amber sparks are missiles in the air, pips are your rails, the amber number is the cannon belt. Centre MFD — artificial horizon. Right MFD — the island map, or the target camera (M). Caution letters on the struts: Fuel, Stall, Terrain, Lock.

; THE LOOP, flown properly this time: trim level at 1000 m, burner to
; 600 km/h, then full back stick and gamma (gpi) sweeps the circle.
; The first attempt taught two things: an untrimmed burner climb loops
; BY ITSELF, and a loop begun at 400 m ends in the island.
wait 10
tap SPACE 3
wait 5
press PLUS
wait 70
release PLUS
until vel >= 1600 900
press DOWN
wait 12
release DOWN
until alt >= 16000 1200
say trim level
press UP
wait 26
release UP
tap MINUS 10
wait 40
say burner - wind her up
tap A 3
until vel >= 2800 700
say PULL
press DOWN
until gpi >= 32 200
shot p_up45
until gpi >= 58 200
shot p_vertical
until gpi >= 116 300
shot p_overtop
wait 8
shot p_top2
until gpi >= 188 300
shot p_downhill
until gpi >= 248 400
release DOWN
press UP
wait 20
release UP
tap A 3
wait 10
shot p_out
say done
end
